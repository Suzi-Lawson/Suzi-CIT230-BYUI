The Art of Relaxing

by Suzi Lawson

Everybody loves to relax. There are many ways to relax in the town of Preston. In the winter there is cross country skiing and snow mobiling. In the Spring there is golfing, fishing, boating, water-skiing and so much more. However, the best part of Preston is the Maple Grove Hot Springs. There is nothing like relaxing in the natural therapy pools and enjoying food from the patron kitchen. You can even bring home something to remember it by from the gift shop.
